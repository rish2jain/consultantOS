# ConsultantOS - Go-to-Market Plan

## Executive Summary

This go-to-market plan outlines the strategy for launching ConsultantOS, an AI-powered consulting platform that democratizes access to McKinsey-grade strategic frameworks and analysis capabilities. ConsultantOS addresses the significant $460K pricing gap between basic AI chatbots and traditional consulting engagements by delivering 60-80% of Big 4 consulting quality at 99.99% cost reduction. Our primary target market consists of independent strategy consultants and boutique consulting firms who need to compete with major consulting houses while maintaining operational efficiency. Through a subscription-based model starting at $49/month, we aim to achieve $150K+ ARR within 12 months by serving 255+ customers.

## Market Analysis

### Market Size and Opportunity

The consulting technology market presents a substantial opportunity across multiple sectors:

* **Business Intelligence Market**: $47 billion (2025 projection)

* **Strategic Consulting Market**: $35 billion globally

* **Independent Consultant Population**: 50,000+ professionals in US/UK markets

### Market Gap Analysis

A critical pricing and capability gap exists in the current market:

* **AI Chatbots**: $80/month with limited strategic consulting capabilities

* **Traditional Consulting Firms**: $500K/project with comprehensive strategic frameworks

* **ConsultantOS Position**: $49-499/month delivering McKinsey-grade frameworks

This represents a $460K opportunity per engagement for mid-market solutions that bridge basic AI tools and expensive consulting services.

### Target Market Characteristics

**Primary Market: Independent Strategy Consultants**

* **Profile**: Ex-Big 4 consultants operating solo or in boutique firms (2-5 people)

* **Annual Billing**: $100K-300K per consultant

* **Time Allocation**: 60% spent on research and analysis activities

* **Geographic Focus**: United States and United Kingdom markets

**Secondary Market: Boutique Consulting Firms**

* Small to medium consulting practices

* Firms seeking to scale without proportional staff increases

* Organizations competing against larger consulting houses

### Jobs-to-be-Done Analysis

Independent consultants hire ConsultantOS to:

1. **Enhance Credibility**: Access McKinsey-grade frameworks to compete with Big 4 presentations

2. **Increase Efficiency**: Reduce research time from 32 hours to 2 hours per engagement (95% reduction)

3. **Cost Optimization**: Avoid hiring expensive analysts while maintaining deliverable quality

4. **Scale Operations**: Handle more clients without proportional resource increases
