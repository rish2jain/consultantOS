# Billing & Subscription FAQ

## General

**Q: Is there a free plan?**
A: Yes! Free tier includes 5 analyses per month, 2 monitors, and Porter's 5 Forces + SWOT frameworks.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit/debit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay via ACH or invoice.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes. We use Stripe for payment processing. We never store your card details.

**Q: Can I get an invoice?**
A: Yes. Invoices are emailed automatically and available in your billing dashboard.

## Subscription Management

**Q: How do I upgrade my plan?**
A: Go to Settings → Billing → Upgrade. Choose your tier and complete Stripe checkout.

**Q: Are upgrades prorated?**
A: Yes. You're only charged for remaining days in the billing period.

**Q: How do downgrades work?**
A: Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep Pro access until then.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, with no cancellation fees. Access continues until period end.

**Q: What happens after I cancel?**
A: You're downgraded to Free tier. Your data is preserved for 90 days for re-activation.

## Usage & Limits

**Q: What counts as an "analysis"?**
A: Each company report generated counts as 1 analysis, regardless of frameworks used.

**Q: What if I run out of analyses?**
A: You'll get a notification at 80% usage. At 100%, upgrade or wait for monthly reset.

**Q: When does my usage reset?**
A: On your billing date each month (e.g., if you subscribed on the 15th, resets on 15th).

**Q: Can I purchase additional analyses without upgrading?**
A: Not currently. Consider upgrading to Pro for 10x more analyses.

## Enterprise

**Q: What's included in Enterprise?**
A: Unlimited usage, white-label, dedicated account manager, 4-hour SLA, custom integrations.

**Q: How is Enterprise priced?**
A: Custom based on team size, usage, and requirements. Contact sales for quote.

**Q: Is there a minimum contract?**
A: Typically 1-year minimum for Enterprise. Contact sales to discuss.

**Q: Can we self-host?**
A: Yes, available for Enterprise customers. Contact sales.

## Promo Codes

**Q: Where do I enter a promo code?**
A: At checkout, click "Have a promo code?" and enter it before payment.

**Q: Can I use multiple promo codes?**
A: No, only one promo code per subscription.

**Q: Do promo codes work on renewals?**
A: Depends on the code. Most apply to first payment only.

## Refunds

**Q: What's your refund policy?**
A: 7-day money-back guarantee on first Pro subscription, no questions asked.

**Q: How long for refund processing?**
A: 5-7 business days back to original payment method.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I cancel mid-month?**
A: No, but access continues until period end.

## Security & Data

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
A: Analysis history preserved for 90 days. Permanently deleted after that.

**Q: Can I export my data before canceling?**
A: Yes, export all reports as JSON/PDF from your dashboard.

**Q: Is my financial data encrypted?**
A: Yes, all data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

A: Enterprise customers only. Pro and Free users: email/chat support.
