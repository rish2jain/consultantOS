## How to Upgrade

### From Free to Pro
1. Go to `/billing` in your dashboard
2. Click "Upgrade to Pro"
3. Enter payment details in Stripe checkout
4. Pro features activate immediately

### From Pro to Enterprise
1. Contact sales at enterprise@consultantos.com
2. Schedule demo and requirements discussion
3. Receive custom quote
4. Sign agreement and get activated

## Billing

### Payment Methods
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit cards
- ACH (Enterprise only)

### Billing Cycle
- Monthly subscriptions bill on same day each month
- Annual subscriptions bill yearly
- Usage resets at start of each billing period

### Refunds
- 7-day money-back guarantee on first Pro subscription
- No refunds on partial months
- Contact support for exceptional circumstances

## Cancellation

### How to Cancel
1. Go to `/billing`
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access continues until period end

### After Cancellation
- Analysis history preserved for 90 days
- No new analyses can be created
- Can re-subscribe anytime
- Data permanently deleted after 90 days
