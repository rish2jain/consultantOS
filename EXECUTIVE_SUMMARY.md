## What's Wrong Right Now?

### Expert Panel Diagnosis (Porter, Christensen, Drucker, Meadows, Godin, Collins)

**Five Critical Blindnesses**:

1. **Context-Free Analysis** (Porter)
   - Problem: "Supplier Power: 3.5/5" is meaningless without knowing industry average
   - Impact: Users can't assess relative competitive position

2. **Missing Disruption Signals** (Christensen)
   - Problem: Tracking current performance, not asymmetric threats
   - Impact: Blindsided by smaller competitors with different business models

3. **Activity Without Results** (Drucker)
   - Problem: Comprehensive data dumps, no clear "now what?"
   - Impact: Analysis doesn't convert to action (<10% action rate)

4. **Component Focus, Not System Aware** (Meadows)
   - Problem: Treating competitive landscape as independent variables
   - Impact: Missing high-leverage intervention points (feedback loops)

5. **Snapshot Mentality** (Collins)
   - Problem: Point-in-time analysis despite continuous monitoring capability
   - Impact: Missing compound advantage building (flywheel effects)

**The Central Issue**: Collecting everything, amplifying nothing. Presenting data, not delivering decisions.
